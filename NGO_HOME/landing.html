About Us

What Is Yuva Sanvedana?

Welcome to Borcelle, where we are dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of underprivileged students across the globe.

Our Vision

"To create a world where every underprivileged student has equal access to quality education, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future."

Our Mission

"Our mission is to empower underprivileged students with tailored education and holistic support, fostering positive societal change through collaboration and nurturing talents."

Our Services

Skill Development Workshops

Conduct workshops to enhance students' skills in areas like coding, language proficiency, and soft skills.

Scholarship Programs

Provide scholarships to deserving students to support their education.

Volunteer Opportunities

Encourage volunteers to join your efforts and contribute their time and skills to support underprivileged students.

Volunteer Management

Efficiently manage volunteer applications, assignments, schedules, and communication.

Student Feedback

Collect feedback from students to improve our programs and services.

Partnerships with Institute

Collaborate with Institute to provide additional resources and support to students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
